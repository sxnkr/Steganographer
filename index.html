What is Steganography?

Steganography is the practice of concealing a secret message or information within another file, such as an image, audio, or video, without arousing suspicion. It is often used to protect sensitive data or transmit covert messages securely. Steganographic techniques involve embedding the secret information in a cover file, making it imperceptible to casual observers. The hidden information can only be extracted by those who know where and how to look for it.
